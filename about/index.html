Section 3 Why Different + Who We Serve
Why Sycomore is different

Impact-Provenance Originals (IPO Art™)

A world-first scoring system that shows how much impact each artwork has generated.

Collections With Purpose

Every artwork belongs to a cause-driven collection like oceans, forests, soil, endangered species and all funding targeted solutions.

Transparent Legacy

Every purchase is tied to visible, trackable impact, creating trust and long-term value.

Unlike traditional art, every Sycomore piece builds a legacy. With every collector, every sale, and every partnership, the Impact Provenance Original™ Score rises, showing the growing footprint of positive change.

Each Sycomore collection consists of natural art pieces. Each piece and the rightful owner is recorded on the Sycomore ledger and comes with a certificate of ownership. This means your artwork will be mapped to the environmental problem and its subsequent solutions, which are ledgered on the Sycomore platform.

Section 5 Manifesto + Contact CTA

It's ok, we're a tech start up. Our roadmap is exciting. So far we are part of the London Climate Action week & contribute to the UN's SDG's.

Take the initiative… Collaborate with us.

Contact
Our challenges are vast. Don't be complicit onlookers.

There is no more time for the awareness narrative but instead we need to be doing. We can't equivocate on this. We're an open platform where readers can explore and engage with problems and solutions to our environmental problems. Here, expert and undiscovered voices dig deep into solutions, small and big and allow readers to be part of the story.

We're creating a new funding mechanism for innovators who can create or co-curate solutions and deliver them successfully. One that supports nuance and complexity in an inclusive and transparent way. One that allows the public and businesses to confidently engage and help fund the best solutions.

It's a place for everyone and fosters deeper connections between the science community, the general public and businesses allowing us all to be part of the solutions.

Together we can collaborate and build a trusted and vibrant ecosystem fuelled by important solutions and the people who create them and those that can help accelerate them into being.
