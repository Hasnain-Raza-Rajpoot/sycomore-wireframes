We are at a critical moment.

The spread of misinformation is undermining the integrity of our systems and creating confusion where clarity is urgently needed. In the face of these challenges, we must ground ourselves in what is real, what is true, and what is meaningful — this is how we begin to solve the complex problems before us.

To protect our planet and ensure a sustainable future, we must return to the fundamentals of knowledge — rooted in facts, truth, and shared understanding. It's only through this solid foundation that we can move forward with purpose.

At Sycomore, we are building a bridge between science and art to create powerful, accurate solutions. Our scientists work directly with artists to provide clear insights into the challenges we face and the potential pathways to solutions.

Our scientists also play a crucial role in guiding where we focus our resources. By co-curating project briefs, validating proposals, and assessing every initiative before we commit funding, they ensure that every effort we make is as effective and meaningful as possible.

If you are a scientist looking to earn extra income and committed to making a tangible difference, we invite you to join us. Please send your CV and covering letter to info@sycomo.re
